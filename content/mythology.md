# The Mythology

**The Seed:** A letter arrived addressed to someone who won't be born for forty years.

---

## Book One: The Arrival

### Chapter I: The Dusty Study

In the beginning of each beginning, there is the Study of Accumulated Silence — a room where dust settles in liturgical layers and drawers hold the patience of decades. Here the Amnesiac awakens, hands already trembling with a knowledge the mind has not yet received.

### Chapter II: The Correspondent

There exists one called the Correspondent, who dwells in the Fold — that place where time doubles back to touch itself. The Correspondent writes letters not to those who are, but to those who *will be*, and in this way creates the very recipients they address. Their handwriting changes under different lights: steady in darkness, trembling in sun, and in candlelight, it becomes a name that cannot be spoken aloud.

### Chapter III: The Letter

The envelope bears the yellow of four decades' waiting. Its postmark reads from a town that maps cannot find, dated in a year that the calendar skipped. The Letter is not prophecy — it is the original sound, and the Amnesiac is its echo, traveling backward toward the mouth that spoke.

*What the Letter contains has not yet been revealed.*
